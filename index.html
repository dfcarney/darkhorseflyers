Flyers Racing Team and Cycling Club

Since 2008, the Dark Horse Flyers racing team and club have been at the front of competitive and development cycling in Ontario. Through the participation of its members, we continue to push our limits in bike racing, triathlon, and charity rides.

The club is intended for road, cyclocross, and mountain bikers, as well as triathletes. It is open to people who are beginners (but who are active and have a decent fitness level) as well as racers. We are an OCA affiliated club.

Both men and women are welcome. Our group rides are organized based on ability, ensuring that everyone has an enjoyable and safe time on the road.

How to Join

  • Review and complete our Risk Management Plan and the OCA Waiver.
  • Turn in the signed waiver to a ride leader or email a scanned copy to flyerspayment@gmail.com.
  • Pay the seasonal club membership fee of $60 by sending an email money transfer to flyerspayment@gmail.com. This fee includes insurance with the OCA, which is required to participate in any club ride or event. This non-refundable fee is reduced to $20 for those with valid proof of a current, full UCI license.

Please note that the club reserves, at its sole discretion, the right to reject or cancel memberships at any time with cause. Reasons include, but are not limited to: overdue membership fees; incomplete/invalid membership forms; and hazardous cycling (i.e. endangering yourself and/or others). Processed membership fees will not be refunded.

A portion of the membership fee is donated to Trails Youth Initiative, which the Flyers have been supporting since 2009.
