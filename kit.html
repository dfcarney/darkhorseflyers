2012 Team Kit

The team kit (jersey and shorts) is here!

Most people liked the Hincapie stuff so we’re staying with it this year and the updated design Angela did looks amazing. If you are new to the club or didn’t get a kit last year, visit the Hincapie website and check out the Velocity jersey and shorts. Their sizing charts are farily comprehensive.

We also have cycling caps, socks, and water bottles. The caps are made by Walz and are a three panel, one-size-fits-all design. Socks are DeFeet Aireators with a 3” cuff and come in S/M/L/XL. The water bottles are 21oz by Specialized.

Pricing is as follows:

  • Jersey $125
  • Bib Shorts $150
  • Caps $20 ($25 non-member)
  • Socks $12 ($15 non-member)
  • Bottles $5 ($7 non-member)

Send an email with your full name, size, and quantity of each item you’d like. We have set up an account so that you can do an email Interac transfer to this address right from your banking website. Most major banks offer this service and it is very secure. Orders are performed in bulk, so while some items might be readily available, you may have to wait for others to arrive.
